Streamer Idle Simulator is a small browser-based incremental game where you grow views, subs, and money over time.

How it works:

  • Click Go live to gain a burst of resources.
  • Buy upgrades to increase your passive income per second.
  • Complete quests, unlock achievements, and progress through seasons.
  • Your progress is saved locally in your browser.

Progress saving: The game stores save data in your browser using localStorage. This means progress is saved on your device and can be cleared by using Reset or clearing site data.

Leaderboards: If you submit a score, the only information sent is the nickname you enter and your score/time.
