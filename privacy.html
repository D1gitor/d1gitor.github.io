This Privacy Policy explains what data is stored when you use Streamer Idle Simulator.

1) Local game save

The game saves progress locally in your browser using localStorage. This may include things like views, upgrades, streaks, and settings.

2) Leaderboard submissions

If you submit a score to the leaderboard, the information sent can include:

  • Nickname you enter
  • Score/time value
  • Game type (views/prestige/speedrun) and weekly/all-time mode

No email address is required by the game to submit.

3) Cookies / ads

The game itself does not require cookies to function. If advertising is enabled in the future, advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to provide and measure ads.

4) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Leaderboard data exists to show rankings and scores.

5) Contact

If you have questions, please use the contact page: Contact.
